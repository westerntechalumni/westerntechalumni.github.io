About Western Tech Alumni

The Western Tech Alumni host workshops for students about how to get a job, run an interview prep program, and use the alumni network to help connect students with the opportunity to do work that they love.

While our events have been sponsored in the past by Western University's Computer Science and Electrical and Computer Engineering departments, Western Tech Alumni is not an official Western affiliate organization.

Joining as a student

Opportunities abound for resume review, career tips, mock interviews, and mentorship from experienced alumni and senior students. Sign up through our Google Form.

Joining as an alumni

If you are looking for a job, sign up through the student intake form.

If you've already found work that you love, and are willing to provide encouragement, advice, mock interviews or potential referrals to your company, please sign up through our alumni form, or contact us directly.
